Project Timelines & Process

Project duration varies based on scope and complexity:

  • Small electrical installations: 1-3 days
  • Security system installation: 2-7 days
  • Residential construction: 2-6 months
  • Commercial buildings: 4-12 months
  • Infrastructure projects: Varies by scope

We provide detailed timelines with every quote and keep you updated throughout the project.

  1. Initial Consultation: Discuss your needs and project scope
  2. Site Assessment: Visit the location if required
  3. Quote Preparation: Detailed pricing and timeline
  4. Contract Signing: Formal agreement and deposit
  5. Planning & Permits: Obtain necessary approvals
  6. Execution: Complete the work with regular updates
  7. Quality Check: Thorough inspection and testing
  8. Handover: Final walkthrough and documentation
